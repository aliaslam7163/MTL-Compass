You can apply for a work permit after you've completed your medical exam and Immigration, Refugees, and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) has received your results.

Call IRCC to ask if they have received your medical results. Then fill out work permit forms.

You must fill out work permit forms (IMM5710 and IMM556) as well as photocopies of:

IMM1442 immigration document Date of your IRB hearing Welfare slip of last welfare check Passport

It is strongly advised that you send your application online, but you can also send your completed forms and documents to Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada.

Mail work permit application to:

IRCC, Case Processing Centre - Work Permit

6212 55th Ave, Unit 55, Vegreville, AB, T9C 1X6
